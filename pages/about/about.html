About Arundel Computer Science Club

Welcome to our club!

Firstly, thank you for visiting our website! Anybody with any interest computer science is welcome into our club. Arundel Computer Science club is all about openness and learning. If you learn something while here, you're doing it right! Our club mainly focuses on group projects with students, which allow them to gather real life experience on various subjects. This website is an example of one!

We have a little blog detailing our activities when we meet every friday, be sure to check that out for updates as to what we are doing!

Our Projects section has information on our various ongoing projects! Click on that if you want to know more!
